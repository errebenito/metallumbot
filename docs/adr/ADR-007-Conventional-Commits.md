## ADR-007: Conventional commits

**Status:** accepted  
**Date:** 12/04/2026  

### Context
We want to convey information about the type of change in the commit message in a concise, standard manner.

### Considered options
Conventional commits vs. verbose commit messages.

### Decision outcome
Chosen option: Conventional commits, due to being a standard way of communicating the type of change and its potential effects. Do note that older commits do not follow this decision as it hadn't been adopted yet.
